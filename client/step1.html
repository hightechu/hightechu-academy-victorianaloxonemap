Call emergency services by pressing the button below. Not sure if someone is overdosing? Look for these signs:

  • They aren't moving and cannot be woken
  • Choking, gurgling noises, or snoring
  • Breathing is slow or shallow
  • Clammy and cold skin
  • Blue lips and nails
  • Tiny pupils
